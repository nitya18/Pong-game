HOW TO PLAY?

This game is named single player pong.Pong is one of the earliest arcade games,it is a ball bat sports game.the aim is to hit as many balls as possible by earning a higher score.The player controls an in game paddle by moving it horizontally across the left,right side of the screen,and has to compete against a computer controlled balls.players use the pads to hit a ball back and forth.the aim is for each player to reach get many points.
