HOW TO PLAY?

This game is named pong.Pong is one of the earliest arcade games,it is a ball bat sports game.the aim is to defeat an oppenent in a simulated table tennis game by earning a higher score.The player controls an in game paddle by moving it vertically across the left side of the screen,and can cpmpete against either a computer controlled opponent or another player controlling a second paddle on the opposing side.players use the pads to hit a ball back and forth.the aim is for each player to reach eleven points before the opponent points are earned when one fails to return the ball to the other.
